**Referee's claim**: The heading "waiting value versus preemption pressure" suggests a discussion of the duopoly preemption mechanism, but the text focuses on Dario's dilemma and asymmetric belief costs.

## Assessment

**Relevant**: Yes, but minor. The heading-text mismatch could confuse readers.

**Needs addressing**: Yes, with a minor edit.

## Analysis

1. Current cash flow versus future capability (inference vs training)
2. [Some other tension]
